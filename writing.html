Nepal Skin Bank Project

Most of my recent writing is on the topic of skin donation in Nepal. I'm part of a team working to raise awareness about Nepal's first Skin Bank, which saves lives by using donor skin grafts to save severe burn victims.

Grants

Here's a list of some grants (thanks, Stanford!) that I worked on in Brazil, Mexico, London, and Sri Lanka.

Chappell Lougee Grant

A grant given to interview olympians that placed 4th in the London Olympics on their reactions and insights.

Pessoa Trejos Grant

A grant given to investigate how Rio de Janeiro was preparing for the impending World Cup and Olympics through the lens of an urban circus school.

Small Grant

A grant given to explore Donald Davidson's view of metaphors as forces through aerial hoop choreography and experimental audience interaction.

Vice Provost for Undergraduate Education Grant

A grant given to research the effects of rising ocean temperatures on mudskippers in Southern Sri Lanka.

SPARK! Grant

A grant given to photograph students in a social circus program in Rio de Janeiro.

Scholastic Portfolio Gold Award

I did this when I was seventeen and it's still the most impressive thing I've done. The Gold Award is given to 10 graduating seniors to honor their writing. See more in the book below and the "Past Life" section.
