Circus

I've run away from several things and thusly joined several circuses.

Specifically, I've trained with two Latin American circuses (Crescer e Viver and GravityWorks) and at various locations scattered across the U.S. Crescer e Viver is a circus in Cirque du Soleil's international network Cirque du Monde and is located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Gravity Works is an aerial troupe based in San Miguel de Allende, Mexico.


Aerial Hoop

Aerial hoops are big, steel rings that spin in the air some number of feet (nine, in my case) off of the ground.

Aerial Silks

Aerial silks are two long strands of fabric that you climb, and then proceed to tie, tangle, drop or pose yourself in and among.

Everything Else

I also like to juggle, unicycle, and tumble poorly (I took intermediate gymnastics at Stanford _eleven_ times).
